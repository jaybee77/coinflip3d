Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Coin Flip 3D Simulator (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email or phone number
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Photos, videos, or audio
  • Motion data
  • Advertising or tracking information

The App operates fully on your device and does not transmit any information to servers or third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, SDKs, or data-tracking tools.

3. Children’s Privacy

Because the App collects no data, it complies with applicable children’s privacy laws and can be safely used by individuals of all ages.

4. Changes to This Policy

If the handling of information ever changes, this Privacy Policy will be updated and the “Last updated” date will be revised.

For assistance, questions, or support, please visit Contact Support.
